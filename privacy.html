Information this store collects

Information may include your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, order details, account details if you create an account, newsletter signup, and messages you send to Aromatic Designer Works.

Payment card information is entered on Stripe’s secure checkout. This storefront does not receive or store your full card number.

How information is used

  • To process, confirm, deliver, and support orders.
  • To respond to questions, returns, and order problems.
  • To operate and secure customer and administrator accounts.
  • To send store news when you choose to join the email list.
  • To prevent abuse, troubleshoot problems, and maintain the store.

Service providers

The store uses Stripe to process payments and Supabase to provide database, authentication, file storage, and store functions. These providers process information needed to deliver their services under their own privacy and security terms.

Information is not sold by Aromatic Designer Works.

Browser storage

The storefront uses your browser’s local storage to remember the items in your shopping bag. You can clear this information through your browser settings; doing so will empty the saved bag on that device.

Product reviews

If you submit a review, the name, rating, title, and review text you provide may be published after moderation. Do not include private information in review text. Pending reviews remain visible only to store administration.

Retention and security

Information is kept for as long as reasonably needed to complete transactions, maintain business records, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations, and operate the store. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no internet service can promise absolute security.

Your choices

You may choose not to create an account and can shop as a guest through Stripe checkout. You may decline the newsletter or ask to stop future marketing messages. To ask about information associated with you, contact the store using the details below.
