Where we ship

Aromatic Designer Works currently ships only to addresses within the United States. Orders submitted with a shipping destination outside the United States cannot be completed.

Processing and delivery timing

Please allow 1 business day for your handmade order to be prepared. Standard delivery is estimated at approximately 5–7 business days after preparation.

Delivery dates are estimates, not guarantees. Carrier delays, severe weather, holidays, and an incorrect address can affect arrival time.

Shipping cost

Standard U.S. shipping is free when the merchandise total is $75 or more after automatic discounts. A flat standard shipping charge is shown at Stripe checkout for smaller orders. Taxes, when applicable, are calculated separately.

Check your shipping address

Customers are responsible for entering a complete and accurate delivery address. If you notice a mistake, contact Aromatic Designer Works immediately. An address change cannot be guaranteed after an order begins processing.

14-day return policy

A return request must be made within 14 calendar days of delivery. To be eligible, every returned product must be unopened and unused. There are no exceptions to the unopened-and-unused condition.

Before sending anything back

Email adw.com1660@gmail.com with your order details and reason for the request. Return instructions will be provided after eligibility is confirmed. Products sent back without first contacting the store may delay review of the return.

Refund timing

Eligible returns are inspected after they arrive. Approved refunds are sent to the original payment method. Your bank or card provider may need additional time to post the credit.

Damaged, incorrect, or missing items

Inspect your order when it arrives. If an item is damaged or the wrong product was sent, contact the store promptly with your order information and clear photos of the product and packaging so the issue can be reviewed.

Need help with an order?

Call 347-423-9364 or email adw.com1660@gmail.com. Aromatic Designer Works is based in Brooklyn, New York.
