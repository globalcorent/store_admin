BURN BEAUTIFULLY & SAFELY

Better care. Better candle.

01

Trim the wick

Trim the wick to ¼ inch before each burn for a calmer, cleaner flame.

02

Build the first melt pool

For wax candles, let the first melt pool reach the edges when possible to help prevent tunneling.

03

Stay candle-aware

Never leave a burning candle unattended. Keep away from children, pets, drafts, and flammable objects; stop after four hours.

QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Know before you glow.

What is the difference between gel and wax candles?

Gel candles have a transparent look that can showcase color and decorative designs. Wax candles have the familiar opaque finish many candle lovers know. Always follow the care instructions supplied with your specific candle.

Are the products really handmade?

Yes. Aromatic Designer Works makes its candles and soaps by hand in small batches. Natural differences in color, placement, or finish can happen from one piece to the next.

How long will my order take?

Orders are prepared in 1 business day. Standard U.S. delivery is estimated at approximately 5–7 business days after preparation.

Can I return a used candle or soap?

No. Returns must be requested within 14 days of delivery and products must be unopened and unused. There are no exceptions to the condition requirement.

How do customer reviews work?

Reviews can be submitted from each product page. Every review is held for moderation before it appears publicly. Aromatic Designer Works does not publish fabricated reviews.
