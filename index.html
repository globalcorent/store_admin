BURN BEAUTIFULLY

Three simple candle-care steps

01

Trim the wick

Trim to ¼ inch before every burn for a cleaner, steadier flame.

02

Let it pool

On the first burn, let wax melt to the edges to prevent tunneling.

03

Burn safely

Keep within sight, away from drafts, and burn for up to four hours.
